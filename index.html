My Mission Here to change the world; serving you agritech right here from Aotearoa. My fundamental belief and calling is to lead and shape the global food biotechnology sector, to minimise the detrimental environmental, social and economic effects of intensified food production and manufacturing. I truly believe there is a need and opportunity to integrate increasing biotechnological advancements into both current and emerging foodstuffs, ingredients and sub-industries. Here to lead the future of innovation and ingenuity in AgriTech and biotechnologies.

Who is Jade Chia? Jade Chia is an assertive, independent and motivated woman, a dreamer who doesn’t shy from challenges, and despite the outcome, takes away learnings from all experiences. Her dream is to facilitate and support equitable food security in Aotearoa. She wants to secure and sustain rural communities for the years to come. What makes her unique is her incredible attention to people, appreciating their individuality, and her empathy. Her acute foresight and passion for a sustainable future. Her aspirations include being a facilitator and voice for those who don’t have the means, those who are underrepresented or don’t have accessibility; creating sustained equality for the disadvantaged in any field. Disrupting and initiating meaningful change.
