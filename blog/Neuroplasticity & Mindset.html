Neuroplasticity & Mindset

What is Neuroplasticity?

Neuroplasticity is the concept of the brain continually growing and evolving in response to experiences. Plasticity implies this process is dynamic and can overwrite bad habits, behaviours and create and improve new skills, bringing about new abilities. Neuroplasticity is vital in learning, improving and developing skills.

Using Neuroplasticity to Your Advantage

It has been suggested that neuroplasticity:

    Helps you learn new things
    Helps manage depression and anxiety
    Helps you engage in activities more thoughtfully
    Aids recovery from strokes and other traumatic brain injuries
    Increases brain volume and memory

In this way, understanding the benefits of neuroplasticity and how to promote it will help you with the above.

Increase Your Neuroplasticity

One of the suggested ways to increase neuroplasticity is to enable the brain through aerobic exercise. This releases hormones, which stimulate synapses and the intensity of neurotransmission. Thus, promoting neuroplasticity.

Research also shows that pushing yourself out of your comfort zone i.e. undertaking new challenges also promotes neuroplasticity.
